## Long Description

Publishing a Figma plugin or widget to the Community means tracking down exact asset sizes, creating frames at the right dimensions, and manually placing content. Heavy Publish Prep handles all of that in one click.

**Pick a platform preset:**
• **Figma Community** — creates a 128×128 icon frame, a 1920×960 cover image with your plugin name and tagline pre-filled, and up to 10 screenshot frames at 1920×960
• **GitHub** — creates a 1280×640 social preview frame for your repository open-graph image

**Screenshot templates included:**
All screenshot frames can use one of five templates — a bare frame, a macOS browser mockup with traffic lights, a centered layout with title and caption, an iPhone-proportioned phone mockup, or a landscape iPad tablet mockup. Every template is fully editable grouped layers.

**Bring your own screenshots:**
Upload PNG or JPG images from the plugin UI. They're automatically resized to fit and applied to the screenshot frames — inside the mockup's content placeholder or as the frame background, depending on the template.

**Smart re-runs:**
Already have frames from a previous run? The plugin skips existing frames and only creates what's missing, so you can add more screenshots without disturbing your layout.

**Export in one click:**
"Select All for Export" selects every frame on the assets page so you can go straight to File → Export.

---

**Who it's for:** Plugin and widget developers who want to stop fiddling with asset specs and get back to building.
